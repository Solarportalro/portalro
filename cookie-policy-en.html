Cookie Policy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance browsing experience.

2. Types of cookies

  • Strictly necessary: essential for site operation.
  • Analytical: help understand user interaction.

3. Cookies we use

  • cookie_consent: stores consent for banners.
  • _ga, _gid: Google Analytics (analytical).

4. Managing cookies

You may refuse or delete cookies via browser settings. Continued use implies consent.
